Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Express delivery options are available at checkout.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes! You can return most items within 7 days of delivery. Please review our return policy for details.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website.
